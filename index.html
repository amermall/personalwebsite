About:

I have been in the Information Technology(IT) field for the last 20 years. The journey has allowed me to interact with many different types of technology. During that time, I have always wanted to learn programming. And at times trying to pursue that skill has been proven difficult; whether due to life circumstances or under-estimating the time involved to learning the skill.

As the kids have gotten older and have become more self-sufficient, it seems the right time to pursue this goal. As the kids will be working on their homework, I will be learning to code!. As, I have been going through the pre-work check points for the Flatiron Software Engineering Flex program , I am reminded how hard it can be to learn a new skill, and how much time it can to take to grasp new concepts. It's almost like learning a new language. Actually, while typing this, I remembered from the Programming as a conversation check point; "...the programming language defines the rules of notation or syntax, that are required for it to ask the computer to do work on your behalf...".

Looking forward to starting this journey...and finishing it! All the best to others in the same class!
